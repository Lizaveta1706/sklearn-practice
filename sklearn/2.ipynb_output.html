TF-IDF¶

TF-IDF (Term FrequencyInverse Document Frequency). $D$. $t$ $d \in D$ :

  1. Term Frequency : $$\text{tf}(t, d) = \frac{n_{td}}{\sum_{t \in d} n_{td}},$$ $n_{td}$ $t$ $d$.
  2. Inverse Document Frequency $$\text{idf}(t, D) = \log \frac{\left| D \right|}{\left| \{d\in D: t \in d\} \right|},$$ $\left| \{d\in D: t \in d\} \right|$ , $t$.

(, ) $(t, d)$ : $$\text{tf-idf}(t,d, D) = \text{tf}(t, d)\cdot \text{idf}(t, D).$$

, $\text{tf}(t, d)$ $\text{idf}(t, D).$

$d \in D$ $\bigg(\text{tf-idf}(t,d, D)\bigg)_{t\in V}$, $V$ , $D$.
